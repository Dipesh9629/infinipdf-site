INFini PDF Netlify Deploy Notes

1. Upload this full static-site folder to Netlify.
2. After connecting your custom domain, replace every `https://your-domain.com` value in:
   - robots.txt
   - sitemap.xml
3. In Google Search Console, submit:
   https://your-domain.com/sitemap.xml
4. Test these pages after deploy:
   - /
   - /about
   - /contact
   - /privacy-policy
   - /terms
   - /disclaimer
5. Test the contact form once on the live domain and confirm that emails arrive in Gmail.
