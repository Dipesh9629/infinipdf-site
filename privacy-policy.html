Privacy Policy

How Infini PDF handles visitor data, files, cookies, and ads

This policy explains what information may be collected when you browse Infini PDF, use document tools, contact us, or interact with ads and analytics.

Information We Collect

Infini PDF may collect limited technical data such as browser type, device information, IP address, and usage events to keep the website stable and improve performance.

If you contact us directly, we may receive your name, email address, and the contents of your message.

When file-processing tools are used, selected files are processed for the requested action. If processing happens locally in the browser, files are not uploaded to our servers.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the website, improve tool reliability, detect abuse, answer support requests, and understand how visitors use the platform.

If advertising services such as Google AdSense are enabled, cookies or similar technologies may be used to personalize or measure ads according to applicable policies.

Cookies and Advertising

Infini PDF may use cookies, analytics tools, and third-party advertising technologies to remember preferences, measure visits, and support monetization.

Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's visit to this and other websites.

File Handling and Security

Infini PDF is designed to process files securely. For browser-based tools, files remain on the user's device and are not stored on our servers during processing.

No system can promise absolute security, but reasonable steps are taken to reduce unauthorized access, misuse, or accidental exposure.

Your Rights and Contact

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to access, correction, deletion, or objection to certain data practices.

For privacy-related questions, contact Infini PDF through the contact page before using the service for sensitive or regulated documents.
